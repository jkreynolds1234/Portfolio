Jenna Reynolds

As a recent graduate from UC Davis with a major in design and minor in computer science, I am passionate about human centered design and technology. Combining my design and computer science backgrounds, my skillset is very diverse. I have completed front end, back end, and full stack projects by myself in my undergraduate career. I am eager to continue learning and connecting with others in the field of web design.

Outside of web design, I have a deep interest in social justice. I grew up volunteering at various philanthropies, including starting a partnership program with the San Francisco SPCA and my community charity league. As a graphic design assistant for the Department of Science Outreach at Edgewood College, I enjoyed increasing the accessibility of science education for minority groups in the community.

Today, I am excited to use my background and knowledge to design beautiful websites to help make peoples' lives easier.
